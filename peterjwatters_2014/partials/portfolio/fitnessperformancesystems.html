FPS: CROSSFIT 15

I worked on this project in with an ex-school friend of mine, Dara O'Boyle. He is the owner and founder of a Crossfit training centre in Blanchardstown and needed some help getting a website up and running. He has purchased a 'Website builder' deal on CityDeal but was having difficulty getting it up and running. I helped him out by configuring URL to link to his hosting account and to setup wordpress for him. He had already gotten an account with ElegantThemes and wished to use one of those themes to style the site.

I chose a suitable theme and installed for him, making sure the site worked well across platforms. I populated a lot of initial content and set up the site so he could continue on with it himself. The site has been accessed here. Dara has made a lot of changes to the colour scheme and some UI elements as time went on but a lot of the main functionality remains the same.
