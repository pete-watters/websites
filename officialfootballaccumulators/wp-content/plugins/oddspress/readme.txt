OddsPress is a fully integrated Football Match Betting Odds plugin for Wordpress.

== Description ==

OddsPress makes it easy to add Football odds to your WordPress powered website and profit from the search traffic it will generate. OddsPress can add Football match odds to your site and allow you to select which Football League you want displayed.

Features:

* Automatically inserts football match betting odds into your pages or posts
* Simple tagging system to display odds
* You can select your football league from over 30 football leagues
* Monitized (optional). You can signup for an affiliate account with our odds supplier and profit from new accounts referred from your site.
* Simple templating engine allows you to create/change the design of the odds displayed to suite your site’s theme.
* Built-in caching system ensures that data is served really quickly and does not slow down the display of your pages or posts.

== Installation ==

1. 	Upload the files to wp-content/plugins/oddspress

2.	Go to the web page http://simplepie.org/downloads/ and download the SimplePie Core, unpack the content and Upload the files to wp-content/plugins/simplepie-core
	
3.	Activate the SimplePie Core Plugin

4. 	Activate the OddsPress Plugin

5.	Go to Settings > OddsPress and configure your settings

6. 	Go to your post/page an enter the tag '[oddspress]'.

** Note : OddsPress plugin relies on SimplePie Core and PHP Version 5.0+ **

That's it ... Have fun!
